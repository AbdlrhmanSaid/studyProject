FAQ

1-Where is the university located?

The university is located in the university district of New Borg El Arab, Alexandria Governorate.

2-University admission system?

Admission requires that the applicant must have a high school diploma or equivalent diploma: five years or three years Admission is in order of passing grades and there is a choice of university admission test Measuring computer skills and testing general information in mathematics requires success 60% From the final grade in this test (this system was canceled in 2024)

3-The goals that the university seeks to achieve in education?

Objectives: According to Law No. 72 of 2019, technological universities were established to achieve several objectives, the most important of which are:
* Creating a new integrated path for applied and technological education and training, parallel to the academic education path
* Applying technology and exploiting it for the benefit of society and qualifying graduates to meet the needs of the labor market
* Providing technological education that provides high-quality educational and training services
* Continuous development of curricula and study plans for all stages and levels of study
*Providing technical assistance and administrative advice in the field of technical education and training
